WELCOME TO KAPLAN UNIVERSITY

Committed to Building Practical Career Skills

To fulfill a promise to yourself. To reinvent yourself. To earn a better living doing something you truly enjoy. At Kaplan University, we understand why you’re going back to school. The outcome of your education is our most important concern.

Kaplan University is an accredited provider of online higher education. We offer more than 180 online programs and degrees in a wide variety of fields.

Kaplan University’s programs are designed to provide you with relevant skills for pursuing employment and advancement in fields such as business, criminal justice†, education, health care, information technology, legal studies, and nursing.‡ Our faculty members are experienced practitioners in their fields, and are committed to their professions, to teaching, and to your success.

Learning online does not mean you learn alone; Kaplan University’s interactive format allows you to engage with your professors and classmates through live seminars, one-on-one instructor interaction, discussion boards, and special events.§ Network with others and build teamwork and communication skills that translate from classroom to career. As a student, you have the support of a helpful team of advisors, dedicated to guiding you through to graduation.

We understand that pursuing a degree is a significant investment of your time and money. That’s why we offer numerous opportunities for you to complete your degree faster and save money, too. Some of these include accelerated bachelor’s and graduate degree programs, and credit for approved prior training and work/life experience.§‡ Undergraduate students could be eligible for Kaplan University scholarships; Kaplan University bachelor’s degree alumni may also be eligible to receive a tuition reduction on a graduate program at Kaplan University.

Kaplan University provides the quality education you want, in a format designed to serve yours needs—online, on your time.

Kaplan University is a part of Kaplan Higher Education LLC, which is a subsidiary of Kaplan, Inc., a subsidiary of Graham Holdings Company.
