Sophia Partner School - Walden University

Sophia is a leading online course provider that offers credit-approved courses aimed at helping students save money and time on school by earning credit towards their course requirements online. Their courses are guaranteed for credit transfer into a degree program at 12 featured partner institutions and ACE-credit recommended for transfer at more than 2,000 additional colleges and universities. Sophia’s offers a broad set of intro-level or general education courses that are required by most degree programs.
